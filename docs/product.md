# Product Overview

GovDocs-AI is an AI-powered document assistant that helps users understand U.S. government documents in plain English.

## Initial MVP

The first version focuses on USCIS and immigration-related documents commonly used by international students and immigrants.

Supported MVP documents:

- Form I-20
- Form I-94
- Form I-797 / I-797C
- Form I-766 / EAD card

## Target Users

- International students
- Immigrants
- First-time USCIS applicants
- People who need help understanding government paperwork

## Core User Problem

Government documents are difficult to understand because they contain legal language, unfamiliar form numbers, deadlines, and unclear next steps.

Users often ask:

- What is this document?
- Do I need to take action?
- Is there a deadline?
- What information should I check?
- Where can I verify this officially?

## MVP Value Proposition

GovDocs-AI helps users upload a government document and receive:

- A plain-English summary
- Important dates
- Required actions
- Key identifiers
- Official government resources
- Safety warnings and uncertainty flags

## Product Boundary

GovDocs-AI does not provide legal, immigration, tax, or financial advice.

The product explains documents using the uploaded file and official government sources.
